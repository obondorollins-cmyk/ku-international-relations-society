Vision

To establish a gold standard for diplomatic fellowship where academic knowledge is transformed into professional mastery, creating leaders who navigate global complexities with precision and strategic foresight.

Mission

To foster a high-caliber community of thinkers and diplomacy strategists, bridging academic theory with the practical art of international diplomacy.

Core Pillars

  • Strategic Excellence
  • Global Agility
  • Visionary Statesmanship

Code of Conduct

  • Professionalism in Protocol
  • Respect for Diversity & Sovereignty
  • Excellence in Representation
  • Collaborative Diplomacy

Oversees research initiatives.

Third Director of Research

Manages additional research collaborations.
